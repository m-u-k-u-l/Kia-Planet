The 2022 Kia Seltos is available in two petrol and one diesel engine option. The 1.5-litre Smartstream petrol engine produces 112bhp at 6,300rpm and 144Nm of torque at 4,500rpm. This engine is available in six-speed manual, six-speed iMT, and IVT transmission options. The 1.4-litre Smartstream Turbo-GDi petrol engine generates 136bhp at 6,000rpm and 242Nm of torque between 1,500-3,200rpm. The Turbo engine is available with six-speed manual and 7DCT option. On the other hand, the 1.5-litre CRDi VGT diesel engine generates 112bhp at 4,000rpm and 250Nm of torque between 1,500rpm-2,750rpm. This engine is available in six-speed manual, iMT, and automatic torque converter.
